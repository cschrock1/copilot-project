My To-Do List

    • Enter a task and (optionally) select a due date, then click Add Task.
    • Click a task to mark it as completed.
    • Use the ☰ icon to drag and reorder tasks.
    • Click ❌ to delete a task.
    • Click Clear All Tasks to remove all tasks.
    • The counter shows how many tasks are left.
    • Your tasks are saved automatically.
